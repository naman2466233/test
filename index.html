I am one of the organizers of Hack In The North 3.0, 2018, the largest student-held hackathon in India.

Last year, Hack In The North 2.0, 2017 was successfully held from 23rd - 25th March 2017 at IIIT Allahabad in collaboration with GitHub (Title Sponsor). It was a phenomenal success with the participation of 300+ hackers from IITs, NITs, IIITs and other colleges from all over India. This, with the fact that most of the horde at the fest was in the 17-26 age-group, is convincing proof of its acclaim among youth.

We have Campus Ambassadors in colleges across the nation which makes the reach Pan-India. Hack In The North 2.0, 2017 saw 36 hours of intense hacking which culminated with hackers coming up with innovative projects to solving real-life problems.

ConsenSys, InfotechHub, GitHub, TopCoder, ZCash, Heroku, Balsamiq and Python Software Foundation are already on board with us this iteration as our title, associate, platinum, gold and silver sponsors this year among many other top companies in the world. More than 2000 people from all over the world have already registered for Hack In The North 3.0 so far.
